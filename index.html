Our story

started with a vision: to spice up the mystery adventure genre with something new.

Though mystery adventure games like Phoenix Wright and Danganronpa have thrilling mysteries to solve, their linear nature gives the player no control over how the story progresses.

What if your decisions could change who lives or dies?

What if you could commit the crime, instead of just solving it?

5 minutes after these questions were asked, Choshibon was formed and production on Re:Deceive began.

Our team

comes from across north america and east asia. based in waterloo, canada
 

Though we're a younger team, we have ample experience to back ourselves up. Each of our members comes from a development background, including everything from mobile gaming and VR development.

What brought us together was our shared belief that the best games take a story-first approach. We know great gameplay can make a game engaging, and that games should have enjoyable gameplay, but we believe its the story that makes a game truly unforgettable.
